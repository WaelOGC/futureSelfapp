Frequently Asked Questions

  • How long does it take?
    The entire process takes just a few minutes. Upload your photo, describe your dream, and receive your Future Self portrait and wisdom letter within moments.
  • Do I need an account?
    No account required to get started. You can try the experience immediately. Creating an account lets you save your results and track your progress over time.
  • Can I redo my result?
    Absolutely. You can generate new results as many times as you'd like. Each attempt helps you refine your vision and deepen your understanding of your goals.
  • Is my photo stored?
    We process your photo to generate your Future Self portrait, but we don't store it permanently unless you choose to save your results. Your privacy is our priority.
  • What if I feel overwhelmed?
    It's normal to feel emotional when connecting with your future self. Take your time, and remember this is a tool for growth, not a source of pressure. If you need support, reach out to a professional.
  • Is this free?
    Pricing will be introduced later; for now this is a basic prototype experience. We're focused on building something valuable before we introduce any costs.
