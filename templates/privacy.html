Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information.

Last updated: December 2024

What Data We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly when using our AI services:

  • • Photos and images that you upload for processing through our AI services
  • • Text input including descriptions, goals, dreams, and other content you provide
  • • Contact information when you reach out through our contact forms
  • • Account information if you create an account (email, preferences, usage history)

How We Use Your Data

Your information is used solely to provide and improve our services:

  • • Generate AI outputs: Process your images and text to create the requested outputs (aged photos, wisdom letters, goal plans, etc.)
  • • Improve experience: Understand how you use our services to make them better and more helpful
  • • Communicate with you: Respond to your inquiries and provide support when needed
  • • Maintain security: Protect against fraud, abuse, and unauthorized access

Storage & Retention

We store your data securely and aim to minimize retention periods.

Generated outputs are stored so you can access them later from your account. We keep your data only as long as necessary to provide our services, comply with legal obligations, and resolve disputes.

You can request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us. We will remove your information within a reasonable timeframe, subject to legal requirements.

Third-Party Processing

To generate AI outputs, we use trusted AI infrastructure providers. These providers process your images and text on our behalf to create the results you request.

We work only with reputable providers who implement strong security measures and privacy protections. Your data is processed according to their privacy policies and our agreements with them.

We do not sell your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

Your Rights

You have control over your personal information:

  • • Access: Request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • • Deletion: Request that we delete your personal information
  • • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • • Portability: Request your data in a portable format

To exercise these rights, please contact us through our contact page.

Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

These measures include encryption, secure data transmission, access controls, and regular security assessments. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security. We encourage you to use strong passwords and keep your account information confidential.

Questions About Privacy?

If you have questions about this privacy policy or how we handle your data, please contact us.
